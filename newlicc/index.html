A DISCIPLE-MAKING CHURCH

Teaching you to live powerfully and effectively as a disciple of Jesus!

In New Life City Church, our pastoral staff, lay leaders and congregation are in agreement with Jesus’ statement in Matthew 28:19-20 that our one and only purpose is to make growing disciples – persons conformed to the image of Christ. We define a disciple of Jesus in the following way:

A disciple is one who believes in Christ Jesus as Lord, and is devoted to His teachings in practice, and relying on the Holy Spirit to live a life of love, total obedience, and service to God and neighbors, including raising other disciples for Christ.

The life of a disciple is one of learning and growth with the goal of becoming like the Lord.
